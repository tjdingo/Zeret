ABOUT US

CRAFTING SPACES WITH PRECISION & PASSION FOR OVER 35 YEARS

OUR STORY

Founded by Pastor Medina, Zeret Architecture has been at the forefront of architectural excellence in New York City for over three decades. With a distinguished career spanning 35+ years, Pastor has built a reputation for creating sophisticated, functional spaces that balance aesthetic beauty with practical purpose.

What began as a small studio focused on healthcare facilities has evolved into a comprehensive architectural practice serving commercial, residential, healthcare, and institutional clients throughout the New York metropolitan area and beyond.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

At Zeret Architecture, we believe that exceptional architecture emerges from a deep understanding of human needs, environmental context, and technical precision. We approach each project with a commitment to:

  • Function & Form: Creating spaces that work flawlessly while inspiring through thoughtful design
  • Sustainable Practice: Integrating eco-conscious solutions that benefit both clients and the planet
  • Collaborative Process: Working closely with clients to transform their vision into architectural reality
  • Technical Excellence: Delivering projects with precision, attention to detail, and construction expertise

OUR TEAM

Pastor Medina

Founder & Principal Architect

Pastor Medina brings over 35 years of experience in architecture, specializing in institutional, residential, commercial, and healthcare projects. His expertise encompasses all phases of a project, from schematic design to construction administration, ensuring efficient and effective project delivery.

Mr. Medina began his career focusing on residential and institutional architecture, where he developed a practical and detail-oriented approach to design. Over the years, he has established a strong reputation for delivering solutions that integrate functionality, precision, and aesthetic value. His work spans large-scale developments and detailed renovations, consistently meeting client needs with professionalism.

A registered architect in New York State, Mr. Medina earned a Bachelor of Architecture and Landscape Architecture from the City University of New York (CUNY). His portfolio includes projects for distinguished clients such as Memorial Sloan Kettering, the New York Academy of Medicine, and New York-Presbyterian Brooklyn Methodist Hospital.

Known for his leadership and problem-solving skills, Pastor Medina is committed to delivering high-quality results while fostering positive impacts on the built environment and the communities it serves.

Terez Villeda

Creative Director

As Creative Director at Zeret Architecture, Terez Villeda's goal is to connect with clients emotionally, understanding their vision and translating it into architectural designs that resonate on a deeper level. Her approach bridges brand strategy with spatial design, creating environments that reflect each client's unique identity and aspirations.

A graduate of Rhode Island School of Design, Terez brings an artistic perspective to architectural projects, infusing spaces with both functionality and aesthetic impact. Her never-ending curiosity and passion drive her to explore innovative solutions that push the boundaries of conventional design while remaining grounded in practical needs.

When not transforming spaces for clients, Terez nurtures her creative spirit through music, surfing, and snowboarding. This balance of professional expertise and personal passion enables her to bring fresh perspectives to every project, creating designs that are both meaningful and memorable.
