OUR SERVICES

ARCHITECTURAL SOLUTIONS FOR EVERY SECTOR

WHAT WE OFFER

At Zeret Architecture, we provide comprehensive architectural services across a range of sectors. Our approach combines creative vision with technical precision, delivering spaces that exceed expectations in both form and function.

Each project is approached with careful consideration of the unique requirements, context, and aspirations of our clients. Whether designing a new commercial headquarters or renovating a healthcare facility, we bring the same level of expertise, innovation, and attention to detail.

COMMERCIAL ARCHITECTURE

Our commercial architecture practice creates innovative, functional spaces for businesses that enhance productivity, express brand identity, and create memorable experiences.

  • Office Environments
  • Retail Spaces
  • Hospitality Design
  • Mixed-Use Developments
  • Corporate Headquarters

From conceptual design to construction administration, we guide commercial projects through every phase with expertise and precision. Our designs balance aesthetic goals with practical considerations of budget, timeline, and operational requirements.

RESIDENTIAL ARCHITECTURE

Our residential practice creates living spaces that reflect the unique lifestyles and aspirations of each client, delivering homes that combine beauty, functionality, and comfort.

  • Custom Homes
  • Apartment Renovations
  • Multi-Family Housing
  • Urban Infill Projects
  • Sustainable Residential Design

We believe that homes should be as individual as the people who live in them. Our collaborative design process ensures that each residential project is uniquely tailored to the client's needs while respecting budget constraints and embracing sustainable practices.

HEALTHCARE ARCHITECTURE

With deep expertise in healthcare design, we create healing environments that prioritize patient experience while meeting complex technical and regulatory requirements.

  • Hospitals & Medical Centers
  • Outpatient Facilities
  • Medical Office Buildings
  • Specialty Clinics
  • Rehabilitation Centers

Our healthcare projects emphasize evidence-based design principles that support optimal outcomes for patients and efficient workflows for staff. We balance technical complexity with human-centered design to create spaces that promote healing and wellbeing.

INSTITUTIONAL ARCHITECTURE

Our institutional practice creates educational and civic spaces that inspire learning, community engagement, and institutional identity.

  • Educational Facilities
  • Civic Buildings
  • Cultural Centers
  • Libraries & Museums
  • Community Spaces

We understand the unique challenges of institutional projects, from complex stakeholder requirements to public accountability. Our designs balance symbolic importance with practical functionality, creating meaningful spaces that serve their communities for generations.

ADDITIONAL SERVICES

Interior Design

Comprehensive interior design services for commercial and residential projects, including space planning, material selection, custom furnishings, and lighting design.

Sustainable Design

Environmentally responsible design services that integrate sustainable strategies, energy efficiency, and green building certification guidance.

Master Planning

Strategic planning services for large-scale projects, campus development, urban design, and community planning initiatives.

Renovation & Adaptive Reuse

Specialized expertise in transforming existing buildings for new uses, historical preservation, and revitalizing outdated spaces.

OUR PROCESS

01

Discovery

We begin by understanding your needs, goals, and vision through in-depth consultation and site analysis.

02

Concept Design

We develop preliminary design concepts that explore spatial relationships, aesthetics, and functional requirements.

03

Design Development

The selected concept is refined with detailed drawings, material selections, and technical specifications.

04

Documentation

We prepare comprehensive construction documents that communicate design intent to contractors and authorities.

05

Construction

We provide oversight during construction to ensure the project is built according to design specifications.

06

Completion

We guide the project through final inspections and ensure all elements meet our high standards of quality.
