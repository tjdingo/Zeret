ABOUT ZERET ARCHITECTURE

Zeret Architecture is a sophisticated NYC-based architectural firm specializing in commercial and residential projects. Our brand identity combines modern Nordic-inspired minimalism with a premium approach to convey professionalism, luxury, and timeless design principles.

Founded by Pastor Medina, who brings over 35 years of experience in architecture, we deliver solutions that integrate functionality, precision, and aesthetic value across large-scale developments and detailed renovations.
