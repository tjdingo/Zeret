FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is your typical project process?

Our process typically follows six stages: Discovery, Concept Design, Design Development, Documentation, Construction, and Completion. We guide clients through each phase with clear communication and regular updates.

Do you work outside of New York City?

Yes, while we're based in NYC, we take on projects throughout the tri-state area and have completed select projects nationally and internationally.

How are your fees structured?

Our fees are typically structured as a percentage of construction costs or as an hourly rate depending on the project type and scope. We provide clear fee proposals before beginning any work.

How long does a typical architectural project take?

Project timelines vary greatly depending on size and complexity. A residential renovation might take 6-12 months from concept to completion, while larger commercial projects can take 18-36 months or more.

Ready to start your project?

We're looking forward to learning about your architectural needs and how we can help bring your vision to life.
